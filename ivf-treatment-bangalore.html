What Is IVF Treatment?

IVF is a process in which eggs are retrieved from the ovaries and fertilized with sperm in a laboratory environment. The resulting embryo is then carefully monitored before being transferred into the uterus.

IVF may be recommended in cases of blocked fallopian tubes, male factor infertility, unexplained infertility, age-related fertility decline, or when other treatments have not been successful.

IVF Treatment Approach

Fertility centres in Bangalore typically follow a structured IVF approach that includes fertility evaluation, ovarian stimulation, egg retrieval, fertilization, embryo culture, and embryo transfer.

Treatment plans are personalized based on factors such as age, medical history, and specific infertility causes. Counselling and patient education are also considered an important part of the IVF journey.

Who Can Consider IVF?

IVF may be considered by couples who have been trying to conceive without success, individuals with known fertility conditions, or those advised by fertility specialists after evaluation.

A detailed consultation and fertility assessment help determine whether IVF is an appropriate option for a particular individual or couple.
